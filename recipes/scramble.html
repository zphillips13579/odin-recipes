Scrambled Eggs

You will never get your eggs to look this good

Scrambled eggs are the final test of anyone who would dare to call themselves a chef. Deceptively simple, this dish will take everything you have to perfect. Frankly, I don't think you can do it. You'll just mess it up, probably. You should give up now.

It takes professional chefs centuries to properly execute this recipe. Prepare to be broken.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Strength of Will

Steps

  1. Place a slick, nonstick pan on the stove. Preheat the pan on medium heat. Weep.
  2. Crack two eggs into a bowl. If you get shells in it, don't worry, you won't be a chef for long. Add a splash of milk and beat the eggs.
  3. Coat the heated pan with butter or another oil.
  4. Take a deep breath.
  5. Plunge the beaten eggs into the pan and begin to stir it viciously. Show me what you got.
  6. Stir it faster. Faster. You are not stirring fast enough. Stir Faster.
  7. Pour the eggs onto a plate.
And you're done! Congratulations and enjoy your breakfast. You've earned it.
